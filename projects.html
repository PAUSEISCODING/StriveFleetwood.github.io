Community Projects

Where we come together to make a difference

Strive Fleetwood is guided by a passionate team of trustees who share a commitment to improving the lives of local people and creating opportunities within the community.

Neighbourhood Welcome Hub

Where residents can get advice, access support, and connect with neighbours in a friendly, welcoming space.

Skills and Training Sessions

Free workshops that support adult learning, digital skills, interview preparation, and confidence-building for local people.

Healthier Fleetwood Clinics

Community-led health sessions that promote physical and mental wellbeing, with practical advice and peer support.

Holiday Club and Fun Days

Family-friendly events, holiday activities, and social gatherings that bring people together across Fleetwood.

Community Listening Sessions

Drop-in conversations with residents to shape future services and make sure local voices are heard.

School Partnership Support

Collaborations with local schools to support pupils, families, and community learning opportunities.
