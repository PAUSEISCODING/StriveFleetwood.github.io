More than coffee

Welcome to Strive Fleetwood – a warm, welcoming not-for-profit coffee shop and community hub based within the grounds of Flakefleet Primary School.

Embed Google Map

Dedicated To Community

Since opening our doors in February 2024, we’ve become much more than just a place to enjoy great coffee. Strive is a space where people connect, friendships grow, support is shared, and the community comes together.

A Space to Be You

Whether you’re stopping by for breakfast, meeting friends, working remotely, joining one of our community groups, or simply enjoying a chat over coffee, there’s always a friendly facewaiting to welcome you.

Community First

Strive exists to bring people together. It’s a place to talk, study, laugh, and share moments that turn into memories.

At Strive Fleetwood, everyone belongs.

Driven by kindness

Every cup helps make a difference. Strive proudly supports charitable initiatives and local causes that strengthen our community and give back with purpose.
