Community Projects

Where we come together to make a difference

Strive Fleetwood is guided by a passionate team of trustees who share a commitment to improving the lives of local people and creating opportunities within the community.

Neighbourhood Welcome Hub

Residents can get advice, access support, and connect with neighbours in a friendly, welcoming space.

Skills and Training Sessions

Free workshops that support adult learning, digital skills, interview preparation, and confidence-building for local people.

NHS Health Checks

Free NHS mini health checks taking less than 30 minutes for adults aged 40-74, including:

Height and weight
Blood Pressure
Cholesterol
Blood Sugar

Kids School Holiday Clubs

Holiday activities for children aged 4-12 taking place in easter holidays, summer holidays and christmas holidays including:

Fun and games
Sports and activities
Music and dance
Water Fun
Creative Crafts
Make New Friends!

Community Listening Sessions

Drop-in conversations with fleetwood neighbourhood police, local housing associations and Wyre council for residents to shape local services and share concerns.

Community Wellbeing Bingo

Free to attend, Community Bingo. Taking place every two weeks on a Tuesday starting at 1:30PM

Crafts and Coffee Mornings

Free to attend, Coffee Mornings and crafting sessions to make new friends, socialise and enjoy free tea and toast.

Baby Groups

Encouraging every child to make their mark through painting, scribbling, messy play & more!
