Our Trustees

Guiding Strive with experience, care, and community spirit

Strive Fleetwood is guided by a passionate team of trustees who share a commitment to improving the lives of local people and creating opportunities within the community.

David McPartlin

David is the Headteacher of Flakefleet Primary School. - a school nationally recognised for its creativity, positivity, and community spirit. Often described as “the happiest school in England,” the school reached the finals of Britain’s Got Talent and came close to achieving a Christmas Number 1 single.


David brings ambition, innovation, and a belief that every child and family should be encouraged to “Dare to Dream.”

Dr Mark Spencer

Mark has served the Fleetwood community as a GP for over 30 years and is the founder of the nationally recognised Healthier Fleetwood programme.


His work focuses on community‑led approaches to improving health and wellbeing, empowering residents to shape positive change in their own town. Mark is also Chair of Pride in Fleetwood.

Lucy Burnett

Lucy joined the trustee team in March 2025, bringing additional leadership, educational expertise, and community knowledge.


As Deputy Headteacher at Flakefleet Primary School, Lucy plays a key role in supporting children, families, and wider community initiatives.

Together, our trustees help guide Strive Fleetwood with compassion, experience, and a shared commitment to creating opportunities for everyone.
